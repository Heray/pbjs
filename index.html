Why Prebid.js?

1. No more engineering work for new pre-bid integrations.

2. Prebid.js already minimized latency for you.

Before prebid.js:

  • total latency 1100ms
  • Blocking calls by pre-bid partners

After prebid.js:

  • total latency 250ms
  • All non-blocking parallel calls

3. Turn on/off, A/B test pre-bid partners without touching the actual code.
